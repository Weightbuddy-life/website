BMR CALCULATOR

What is BMR and what is a BMR calculator?

BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) measures the quantity of calories your body burns at rest to sustain vital functions, such as breathing, heart rate, and brain function.

How can using the BMR calculator help you lose weight?

Your BMR can be used to help you gain, lose, or maintain your weight. By calculating how many calories you burn, you can know how many to consume. Basically:

Is your objective to maintain your weight? Consume the same number of calories that you burn.

Is your objective to gain weight? Consume more calories than you burn.

Is your objective to lose weight? Consume fewer calories than you burn.

There are different formulae to calculate this. We use the Mifflin St Jeor formula to calculate your BMR as this is widely considered the most accurate formula.
