BMI CALCULATOR

What is BMI?

BMI, or Body Mass Index, is a formula to determine the ideal body weight range of an individual based on their height. It also indicates if you are underweight, overweight, obese or in the normal weight range.

If I calculate BMI how can I lose weight?

The BMI formula can give adults age 20+ an idea of their healthy weight range. BMI can be used to powerfully motivate you to reach your target weight.

Muscular people should aim for the higher range as muscle is denser than fat.

Remember, BMI is an average and should only be used as a guide. The formula should not be relied upon for: Asian/other ethnic groups, pregnant women, very tall or very short people.
