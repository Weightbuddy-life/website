Need to lose weight now? Weightbuddy is for you!

Hi, I’m Adam (officially Dr Adam Bertram BSc, BEd, MEd, PhD), the founder of WeightBuddy. We’re a start-up founded in February 2021. Our aim is to help people lose weight fast for free.

Why do you want to help people lose weight now?

Because I have been through the struggle to lose weight.

I needed to lose weight and I wanted to do it safely and fast. After some initial research and experimentation, I developed my personal weight loss plan and called it WeightBuddy. This is how I managed my personal weight loss journey. I lost 20 kg in 10 weeks with the programme.

How did you build WeightBuddy?

My decade-long background as an academic and researcher in science education gave me the knowledge to build a programme based on scientific research and evidence-based formulas for quick weight loss.

There are many cool apps and websites to help you lose weight or track your calories, but none had the combined functionality that I needed. So, I built WeightBuddy for myself… and now I’m sharing it with you.

Why are we called WeightBuddy?

Our aim is to provide a full-featured app that offers users to fully achieve their weight, health and nutritional goals. It will provide you with the ability to track and graph your health and weight data and much, much more. Stay tuned!

The WeightBuddy Future

Are you wondering why we are called Weight Buddy? That’s because we hold your hands through your weight loss journey. The struggle to lose weight is tough, and one of the most intimate and sometimes difficult struggles anyone has to deal with. A true buddy of yours will stick by your side through these trying times. And that’s what we want to do.

There are other services that help you lose weight fast, however, no one offers the level of support and gentle nudging we provide throughout the process for free. Our entire WeightBuddy programme is designed to cater to the psychology of humans trying to lose weight.

From measuring your current weight to setting realistic goals to tracking your progress, WeightBuddy is a one-stop-shop. Our role as your weight loss buddy is we want to cut through the noise, make it absolutely easy to follow, and show you how evidence-based science aides fast weight loss.
